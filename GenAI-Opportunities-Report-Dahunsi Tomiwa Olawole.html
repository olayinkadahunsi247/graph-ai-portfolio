Executive Summary

For DAWIMOT GLOBAL LTD, the integration of Generative AI (GenAI) represents a transformative opportunity to scale artisanal quality with industrial efficiency. Operating in the premium market of Ikoyi, Lagos, the company can leverage AI not just for administrative tasks, but as a core engine for culinary innovation, personalized customer experiences, and operational excellence in cake production. This report outlines how to transition from manual "how-to" processes to an AI-augmented production powerhouse.

Company Overview

Client: Dahunsi Tomiwa Olawole
Organization: DAWIMOT GLOBAL LTD
Location: Ikoyi, Lagos, Nigeria
Industry Context: Based on the request for cake production guidance, DAWIMOT GLOBAL is positioned within the high-end confectionery and food services sector. In the competitive Lagos market, differentiation through branding, consistent quality, and rapid response to customer trends is critical.

Top GenAI Opportunities

1. AI-Powered Recipe Engineering

Utilize LLMs to analyze global flavor trends and local Nigerian ingredients to generate unique, proprietary cake recipes (e.g., hibiscus-infused layers or spice-blended sponges) tailored to Ikoyi’s sophisticated palate.

2. Visual Pre-Visualization

Deploy Image Generation AI (like Midjourney or DALL-E) to create high-fidelity 3D renders of custom cakes for clients before production. This reduces physical prototyping costs and increases "bespoke" sales conversions.

3. Smart SOP Generation

Convert basic production steps into comprehensive, multi-lingual Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). GenAI can draft detailed training manuals for staff, ensuring consistency in texture, moisture, and decoration across every batch.

4. Hyper-Local Marketing

Automate the creation of social media content specifically designed for the Lagos market. AI can generate "Naija-centric" copy and localized video scripts that resonate with the luxury demographic of Ikoyi.

5. Personalized Customer Concierge

Implement a WhatsApp-based AI agent that handles complex custom cake inquiries, providing instant quotes, flavor recommendations based on the occasion, and managing delivery logistics in Lagos traffic.

Implementation Roadmap

1
Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4): Digitize existing recipes and "step-by-step" processes. Train a private AI model on DAWIMOT’s specific quality standards and flavor profiles.
2
Phase 2: Creative Pilot (Weeks 5-8): Launch the AI Visual Catalog. Use GenAI to design the "Signature Ikoyi Collection" and showcase these digital renders on Instagram to gauge market interest.
3
Phase 3: Operational Scale (Weeks 9+): Integrate the WhatsApp AI Concierge and use GenAI for predictive inventory management to reduce waste of premium ingredients.
